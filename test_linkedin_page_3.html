      About us

      Copenhagen Fintech is a non-profit, award-winning* innovation hub founded in 2016 in Denmark, with the mission to foster innovation within the financial sector, by building a thriving fintech ecosystem. We support new financial technologies with potential to shape the future, by creating connections throughout all levels of industry - from start-ups to financial institutions, research organisations, governments, NGOs and individuals who want to make a difference. By connecting changemakers within the industry, we foster a thriving innovation ecosystem. We support every step of the development process with tailored offerings, with great success stories of scaled solutions at international level. *ECEI GOLD Label “Excel in Cluster Excellence” in August 2022 _______ Communication activities supported by ”Innovationskraft 23-24: Finans og Fintech“ project, by the European Regional Fund and the Danish Board of Business Development.

          Tryg is looking for Nordic-based health tech entrepreneurs to partner-up and help shape the next generation of proactive health and well-being employer-paid coverages.    ➡️ Co-create with Tryg a service (digital, hybrid or analog) that can enrich their employer-paid health insurance product and turn it into a proactive solution which lifts the overall health and well-being of employees.    ______    The Challenge    🏔 The number of people with private health insurances has been increasing drastically over the past 15 years. Now, close to 4m people in Denmark, Norway and Sweden have access to a private health insurance and the numbers are increasing.    🏔 Private health insurances are by nature reactive and only come into play once an injury or illness has occurred and do very little or nothing to improve general health and wellbeing. Our challenge is to rethink the employer-paid health programs and move them from being reactive coverages to proactive partners in both their professional- and personal life.    🏔 What services (digital, hybrid or analogue) can enrich the health insurance product and turn it into a proactive solution which lifts the overall health and wellbeing of employees?    ______    Application deadline: May 17th 2023  Apply now: lnkd.in/eNsHZdTc   #copenhagenfintech #fintechmatters #nordichealth #globalhealth #futureofhealth  _______________    *The Partnership Fast-Track is a process run by Copenhagen Fintech to enable commercial partnerships by matchmaking tech companies and large organisations for a PoC-focused partnership - to co-create a solution and go to market.
