      About us

      Copenhagen School of Entrepreneurship (CSE) exists to empower students in unleashing their potential by creating, transforming, or simply bringing something new into life through entrepreneurship. Since 2007 CSE, has accumulated knowledge, experiences, and learnings on how to develop as a space for entrepreneurship. Simply drop by our office every Wednesday from 14-16, second floor, to get a chat, or follow us on Facebook to attend our amazing events! We look forward to meeting you!
